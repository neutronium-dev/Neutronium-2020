Neutronium 2020

We, Chlorine, are proud to announce our very first event "Neutronium 2020 | Digital Science and IT Carnival". Failure is a part of learning. We want to encourage the students to fall, to pick themselves up, and to learn from their failures. Science came to its current state through repeated trial and error by the Scientists. Yet nowadays, innovative projects that fail get no recognition at Science fairs while copied projects from the internet that simply work win. A Science/IT fair should prioritize learning and innovation. That's why in "Neutronium 2020 | Digital Science and IT Carnival" Chlorine is doing things in a new way. We are ditching the 1st-2nd-3rd system and changing the whole judgement. Join us now and help us establish this change.

Exhibition of Exhibitions

We are keeping this simple and keeping as much room as possible for creativity. We'll allow projects, wall magazines, ideas, experiments etc. As long as it is innovative and related to Science/IT and can be described or showcased on camera; It will be allowed. The participants will get 3 feedback sessions with the organizers, 2 of which are optional and 1 is mandatory.


There the organizers will give feedback and advice and ask various questions as well. Finally, the participants will be asked to submit a video describing or showcasing their submission in this group. Remember, there won't be any 1st-2nd-3rd but awards will be given. Backspace

The workshop will be held on 21st August at 08:00 PM BST. As for the contest, it isn't a timed event. All you need to do is submit your code by 25 August in this group. You are free to use the internet, libraries, Google, Stack Overflow. Everything is allowed.


You can submit solutions as a group or individually. We will go through your code. This segment is for the coders who are looking for a fun challenge. The challenges are available in the announcements section of the group.

Outline

The workshop will be held on 24th August at 08:00 PM BST. As for the contest, submit your original entry/entries (such as posters, banners, logos, digital artwork etc.) in the Facebook group with the #Neutronium2020, #ChlorineDhalbo and #OutlineSubmission hashtags at the botton of your post. Your post must include your and your submission's name (as well as your submission itself, duh). You can also add some text describing your entry but that's completely upto you. If you want to submit multiple entries, post them as individual posts, instead of adding all of them in one post. (Maximum three entries per person).


You also must email your submission to outline.submissions@gmail.com, otherwise your entry will not be considered. Submit your entry/entries by 25 August, 2020. Don't even dare to copy someone else's work. You will be banned from this event and every other future event of Chlorine.
